Frequently Asked Questions

What is ResumePro?

ResumePro is an iOS app that helps you build professional, ATS-optimized resumes using on-device AI. It includes an ATS Scanner, AI Cover Letter generator, and PDF export.

Is my data safe?

Yes. All AI processing happens entirely on your device. Your personal information never leaves your iPhone.

Free version limits?

Free users get 3 PDF exports and 1 ATS scan. Premium unlocks unlimited exports, ATS scans, AI cover letters, and all future features.

Manage subscription?

Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions on your iPhone, or use "Manage Subscription" in the app's side menu.

Restore purchase?

Open ResumePro, go to the paywall, tap "Restore Purchases" with the same Apple ID.

App not working?

Close and reopen, restart device, update to latest version. Contact us below if it persists.
